About

On this website we provide pointers to the various deep dive books we, or our colleagues, have produced over the last couple of years. These books cover a broad spectrum of topics covered, ranging from the ESXi CPU and memory schedulers to Kubernetes. The idea has always been to provide deep, yet easy to understand, content for people at various stages of their IT/Cloud careers. Provide people that next level of expertise they are looking for. Enjoy.
